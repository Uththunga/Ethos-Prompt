## What is the Marketing Agent?

The Marketing Agent is an intelligent conversational AI designed to help potential customers learn about EthosPrompt's services, pricing, and technical capabilities. It combines:

- **Knowledge Base Search:** Instant access to accurate company information
- **Self-Correction:** Validates responses for accuracy before delivery
- **Context Awareness:** Remembers conversation history for natural dialogue

## Quality Assurance

### How We Ensure Accuracy

Every response is validated through our **reflection mechanism** before delivery:

1. **Hallucination Detection:** Ensures no made-up information
2. **Brand Voice Check:** Maintains professional, on-brand tone
3. **Formatting Validation:** Ensures readability with bullet points
4. **Completeness Check:** Confirms your question was fully answered

If validation fails, the agent automatically regenerates the response with improvements.
