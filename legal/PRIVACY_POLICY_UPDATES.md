## Summary of Changes

## Proposed Additions

### Section: What Information We Collect

**Add new subsection:**

#### 2.6 Performance Caching Data

We collect and temporarily store the following information for performance optimization:

**Automatically Collected:**
- User queries (with PII automatically removed)
- AI-generated responses (generic only)
- Query timestamps and frequency
- Page context (e.g., "pricing page", "services page")
- Cache hit/miss statistics

**NOT Collected in Cache:**
- Email addresses, phone numbers, or contact information
- Credit card or payment information
- Social security numbers or government IDs
- Personally identifiable information of any kind
- User-specific or personalized responses

---

### Section: How We Use Your Information

**Add new subsection:**

#### 3.5 Performance Optimization

We use cached query data to:
- **Reduce Response Time:** Deliver instant responses for common questions
- **Improve Service Quality:** Identify frequently asked questions to improve our knowledge base
- **Monitor Performance:** Track cache effectiveness and system health
- **Optimize Resources:** Reduce unnecessary AI computations

**We DO NOT use cached data for:**
- Marketing or advertising
- User profiling or tracking
- Selling to third parties
- Training AI models on user data

---

### Section: How We Protect Your Information

**Add new subsection:**

#### 4.4 Cache Data Protection

**Security Measures:**
- **Automatic PII Detection:** All queries are scanned for personal information before caching
- **PII Redaction:** Email addresses, phone numbers, and other PII are automatically removed
- **Quality Validation:** Only high-quality, generic responses are cached
- **Encryption:** AES-256 encryption for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit
- ** Access Control:** Cached data accessible only to authorized systems
- **Regular Audits:** Security reviews of caching infrastructure

**Data Isolation:**
- Each user's cache lookups are independent
- Cached responses are generic and not user-specific
- No cross-user data exposure

### Section: Data Retention

**Update existing section or add:**

#### 5.3 Cache Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period | Deletion Method |
|-----------|------------------|-----------------|
| Cached Queries (anonymized) | 30 days | Automatic permanent deletion |
| Cached Responses | 30 days | Automatic permanent deletion |
| Cache Statistics (anonymized) | 90 days | Automatic permanent deletion |

**After Retention Period:**
- All data is permanently and irretrievably deleted
- No backups or archives are maintained
- Deletion is automatic and does not require user request

### Section: Your Privacy Rights

**Expand existing rights section:**

#### 6.7 Cache-Specific Rights

**Right to Know:**
- You can request information about whether your queries are currently cached
- You can view anonymized versions of your cached queries
- You can see cache statistics for your account

**Right to Delete:**
- You can request immediate deletion of all your cached data
- Deletion occurs within 24 hours of request
- No retention after deletion (permanent removal)

**Right to Opt-Out:**
- You can disable caching for your account
- Opt-out takes effect immediately
- Note: Performance may be reduced (no instant responses)

**Right to Access:**
- You can export your cached data in JSON format
- Export includes queries, responses, and metadata
- Available through account settings or support request

**How to Exercise Your Rights:**
1. **Email:** privacy@ethosprompt.com with subject "Cache Data Request"
2. **Account Settings:** Navigate to Privacy Settings > Cache Management
3. **Support:** Contact support@ethosprompt.com

**Response Time:** Within 30 days (typically 3-5 business days)

### Section: Children's Privacy

**Add clarification:**

#### 7.3 Children and Caching

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly:
- Cache queries from children under 13
- Process personal information of children
- Use data for profiling individuals under 13

If we become aware that we have cached data from a child under 13, we will:
1. Immediately delete all cached data
2. Disable caching for that account
3. Notify the account holder
4. Report as required by law (COPPA compliance)
